Dental Insurance & Plans

Maximize your benefits with our cashless network. We partner with India's leading health insurance companies to offer hassle-free claims for your dental treatments.

How Insurance Works

Simple 3-step verification process to check and claim your dental coverage.

1

Check Coverage

Submit your policy card details during registration or upload them securely through our patient portal prior to your visit.

2

Pre-Authorization

Our desk verifies the coverage with your insurer. We provide a breakdown of approved items and any out-of-pocket costs.

3

Cashless Billing

Upon treatment completion, we process the claim directly. You only pay the non-covered co-pay or deductibles.
